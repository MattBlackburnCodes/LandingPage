ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Matthew Blackburn, an IT professional metamorphosing into a frontend web developer with a solid foundation in system administration, network management, and customer support. With a rich history of roles ranging from Senior End User Analyst to Desktop Support Specialist, I bring with me a wealth of knowledge and an innate ability for problem-solving and seamless software/hardware integration.

Transitioning from roles that encompassed overseeing complex IT projects, troubleshooting intricate issues, and fostering collaboration within technology teams, I am keen to channel these experiences into creating user-friendly web experiences. My expertise lies in navigating complex tech environments with proficiency and adapting to the continually evolving dynamics of the tech industry.

While currently immersing myself in the intricate worlds of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and a plethora of other languages and tools, my comprehensive background allows me to approach web development with a unique perspective that integrates both the administrative and creative aspects of technology. My knack for managing projects effectively and facilitating high-level client meetings sets me up to be a notable contributor to multi-disciplinary web development teams.

On the academic front, I have obtained a BS in Computer Science with a focus on Software Engineering from Colorado Technical University, in July 2023. Apart from my formal education, I continually strive to enhance my skills through platforms like The Odin Project and W3 Schools.

My journey has equipped me with an adeptness in various operating systems, networking protocols, and project management methodologies. As I venture into the web development space, I am eager to apply these transferable skills and contribute towards building cohesive, efficient, and user-centric web platforms.

Feel free to explore my GitHub to check out my ongoing projects or connect with me on LinkedIn. I look forward to the exciting challenges and boundless opportunities that the world of web development holds.
